How does the free trial work and what is included?

Free trials provide you with a full version of a Business plan for 14 days. Youll find that the trial period does impose a few temporary limits on the contents, like the number of templates you can use. Other than that, you get the full experience of Favland, so you know exactly what youre getting when you become a Favland customer.

What happens after my free trial ends?

After the 14 days trial your account will be put on hold. If you choose to keep your Business plan, you will need to enter and confirm your payment details. After the confirmation you continue with your account and data as you left it during the free trial.
